# VF Corporation

VF Corporation is a global apparel and footwear company headquartered in Denver, Colorado. The company owns major consumer brands including Vans, The North Face, Timberland, Altra, Eastpak, Icebreaker, JanSport, Kipling, Napapijri, and Smartwool, operating more than 1,500 stores globally.

## Overview

VF Corporation does not offer a public developer API program. Its technology integrations operate through third-party e-commerce platforms and enterprise supply chain systems. VF brands integrate with platforms such as Shopify, Amazon, Magento, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce via third-party integration services. Recent technology investments include deployment of Nedap's Inventory Engine for omnichannel inventory visibility across its brand portfolio.
